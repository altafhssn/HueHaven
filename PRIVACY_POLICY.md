# HueHaven — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-23_

HueHaven is a single-player offline puzzle game. We respect your privacy.
This policy is intentionally short because **there is almost nothing to disclose**.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** HueHaven does not collect, transmit, or share any personal
information.

## What we store on your device

The game saves your local progress to a single config file in the standard
Android app data folder (`user://ball_sort_save.cfg`). This file contains:

- Stars earned per level
- Highest unlocked level
- Settings preferences (colorblind shapes on/off, sound on/off, haptics on/off)

This file stays on your device. We never read it, upload it, or back it up
to any server. Uninstalling the app deletes it.

## Network access

HueHaven does **not** request the `INTERNET` permission. The app cannot
make network requests. It will work offline forever.

## Permissions we request

- **`VIBRATE`** — used to play subtle haptic feedback on key interactions
  (selecting a tube, completing a puzzle). Can be disabled in Settings.

That's the only permission.

## Third parties

HueHaven does **not** integrate any third-party SDKs:
- No analytics (no Firebase, no Google Analytics)
- No advertising (no AdMob, no banner ads, no rewarded video)
- No crash reporting service
- No social-login providers
- No in-app purchases
- No cloud save

## Children

HueHaven is rated for all ages. Because the app collects no personal data
and has no ads, it complies with COPPA and similar regulations by design.

## Changes to this policy

If a future version of HueHaven introduces a feature that requires data
collection (e.g. cloud save or leaderboards), this policy will be updated
**before** that feature ships, and a clear in-app notice will explain
what is changing and ask for your consent.
